Menu

Gelato

gelato is a type of ice cream from Italy. It uses more milk, less cream, and is made without any egg yolks. This cold treat is creamy, decadent, and melt-in-your-mouth delicious. Essentially, it’s richer and denser than other ice cream types. Plus, it has less sugar and is lower in fat, making it perfect for indulging.

Sherbet

It’s a bit fizzy, a little sweet, and a whole lot of fun! This frozen treat comes in different flavors and is perfect if you want sweet-tart combos. It’s made by combining frozen fruits, egg whites, water, and milk or cream. So it’s lighter than traditional ice cream, with a fruity flavor that makes it delicious.

Sorbet

This frozen delight has little to no dairy. Instead, it features a mix of fruit juice or purée, sweetened water, and egg whites

Ice Popsicle

Ice pops contain flavorings, sweeteners, stabilizers, gum, and water. The mixture is then poured into molds and placed in the freezer.

Kulfi

It’s infused with rich flavors and spices like saffron, cardamom, and rosewater, making it unique. The rich, creamy goodness of kulfi makes it perfect if you love indulging in a treat. And it has a satisfying crunch from the nuts that cuts through the dense texture.

Mochi Ice Cream

This Japanese dessert features creamy ice cream coated with sweet pounded glutinous rice dough. It has a chewy texture and melt-in-your-mouth ice cream filling, making it fun to eat. The filling oozes with delightful flavors as you bite into this ice cream. This dessert is typically shaped into balls and comes in different colors and flavors.

Rolled Ice Cream

The ice cream maker pours the mixture into a frozen surface and mixes the flavors. Once the mixture freezes, the ice cream is rolled up and served in a cup. It’s truly magical. The best part about this dessert is that you can get any flavor. The possibilities are endless!

Shaved Ice

It’s made from fine ice shavings drizzled with a flavorful sauce. It can be topped with an array of flavors, from fresh fruits to candies and chocolate.

Falooda

This Indian dessert is a work of art and an explosion of deliciousness. It combines thin vermicelli, jelly, and basil seeds with a creamy base infused with rosewater. This cold treat will surely blow you away with each bite.

Philadelphia Ice Cream

The thing about Philly ice cream is that it’s all about texture. It’s typically dense and creamy, with a rich, satisfying mouthfeel. Made by churning milk, cream, sugar, and flavorings, it’s a simple yet satisfying treat! It comes in so many flavors and pairs perfectly with almost any add-ins!

Italian Ice

The base of this Italian dessert is similar to sorbet, but it doesn’t contain dairy or eggs. Typically served in cups or glasses, it’s great with toppings like candies, chocolate, and fresh fruit.

Soft Serve

It’s sometimes called “whipped ice cream,” or “frozen custard.” Either way, it typically features milk, ice, and flavorings churned with air. It has a smooth, fluffy texture that pairs perfectly with sweet syrups and other toppings.

Caramel Apple PIE Ice Cream

This apple pie inspired ice cream is filled with the flavors of chai spices, granny smith apples, and a rich caramel sauce.The apples are cooked and blended up in the vanilla custard base ice cream to give a smooth, creamy textured ice cream.

Neapolitan ice cream

This is composed of three flavors. They are chocolate, vanilla, and strawberry that are arranged side by side without packaging in between them.

Ice Cream Cake

An ice cream cake is a cake with ice cream as the filling for a swiss roll or a layer cake. A simpler no-bake version can be made by layering different flavors of ice cream in a loaf pan.


Our Special

Peppermint Waffle Ice Cream Sandwiches

A pair of peppermint waffles (made with cake mix! Woop!) squish creamy vanilla ice cream to make a festive homemade ice cream sandwich.

Butter Pecan

Butter pecan ice cream is a rich, buttery, and nutty ice cream flavor. It’s essentially vanilla ice cream enriched with whole pecans toasted in browned butter.

Ube

Ube is used as the main ingredient in many desserts like cakes, cookies, and croissants. It’s often a popular Filipino dessert made with shaved ice, condensed milk, and other ingredients like sweetened beans, coconut strips, and sugar palm fruit.
